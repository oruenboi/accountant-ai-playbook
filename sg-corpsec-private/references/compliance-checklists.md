# Compliance Essentials - SG Private Limited (Unlisted SMEs)

## Annual Cycle
- Fix FYE; align bookkeeping cut-off.
- AGM: hold within 6 months after FYE unless members pass AGM dispensation and circulate FS within 5 months of FYE.
- Annual Return (AR): file via BizFile+ within 7 months after FYE; include XBRL FS unless exempt (small EPC/sole shareholder-director micro entities: upload true PDF FS or declare unaudited if allowed). Refresh shareholder info before filing.
- Director statement for solvency for audited exemption where applicable; board minute + members' resolution for FS adoption/AGM waiver.
- Late AR/AGM: compute penalties, prepare statutory explanations, consider court waiver for repeat defaults.

## Registers & Beneficial Ownership
- Maintain registers: members, directors, CEOs, secretaries, auditors (if any), charges, RORC (controllers), ROND (nominee directors), RONS (nominee shareholders if any), IP of seal (if used), address of records.
- RORC: keep internal register AND lodge to ACRA central RORC via BizFile+. Updates/changes to controller info must be lodged within 2 business days after the internal register is updated; perform annual confirmation with controllers.
- RORC lodgement resumed and must be current from 16 Jun 2025 onward.
- ROND/RONS: lodge nominee director/shareholder info and supporting proofs to ACRA. Existing companies must file by 31 Dec 2025; ongoing changes to be lodged within 2 business days.

## Officers
- Company secretary: appoint within 6 months from incorporation; office cannot be vacant >6 months. Board should minute consent and fit-and-proper check.
- Director/CEO particulars: lodge appointments, cessations, and any change of name, ID type/number, address, nationality, or contact within 14 days via BizFile+.
- Resignation: ensure at least 1 locally resident director remains; if last local resigns, appoint replacement before lodging.

## Share Capital & Ownership
- Share allotment/issue: board approval + members' (if required), lodge \"Return of Allotment\" within 14 days of allotment; update register of members and share certificates.
- Share transfer: e-stamp instrument within 14 days of execution (or 30 days if signed overseas); update register of members and certificate. No separate ACRA filing, but reflect new cap table in the next AR.
- Share buy-back/redemption/capital reduction: follow Companies Act procedures; lodge relevant special resolutions/return of capital forms within 14 days and check solvency statement requirements.

## Registered Particulars
- Registered office address or company name change: lodge within 14 days; update stationery/website/UEN displays.
- Principal activity/FY change: file FYE change only once every 5 years unless for special reasons; do not exceed 18 months first FY without ACRA approval.

## Records & Evidence
- Keep signed board/shareholder resolutions, controller/nominee declarations, ID/address proofs, stamp certificates, solvency statements, engagement KYC, and BizFile+ transaction numbers.
- Retain accounting records 5 years; keep minute books at registered office or notified alternative address.

## Quick Intake Checklist (new client or rescue case)
- Latest BizProfile, cap table, constitution, past AR receipts, FS/AGM papers for last 2 years.
- Confirm FYE, audit status, XBRL eligibility, group structure, beneficial owners & nominees.
- Identify gaps: missing secretary, overdue AR/AGM, unstamped transfers, unsigned resolutions, absent RORC/ROND/RONS lodgements.
- Draft remediation plan: prioritise statutory deadlines (soonest AR/AGM, BO lodgements, officer changes), then governance tidy-up.
