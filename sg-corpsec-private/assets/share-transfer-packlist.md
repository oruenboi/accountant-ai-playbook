# Share Transfer Packlist

Use this checklist before/after lodging a share transfer.

Pre-transfer
- Obtain executed instrument of transfer (form 32A or custom) dated: {{DATE_EXECUTED}}
- E-stamp within 14 days (SG) or 30 days (overseas signing); stamp cert no.: {{STAMP_NO}}
- Board approval if required by constitution/SHA.
- Check pre-emption/ROFR has been cleared or waived.

Registers & Certificates
- Update Register of Members with transfer details (transferor, transferee, certificate numbers, consideration).
- Cancel old certificate no. {{OLD_CERT_NO}}; issue new certificate no. {{NEW_CERT_NO}} dated {{DATE_ISSUED}}.
- Update share certificate log.

Supporting proofs
- ID/address proof of transferee; if nominee, keep nominee declaration and controller details.
- Payment evidence for consideration.

Post-transfer
- Reflect new cap table for next Annual Return.
- If controllers change, update RORC within 2 business days.
- If nominee arrangements exist, lodge ROND/RONS updates within 2 business days.
